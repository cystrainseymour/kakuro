Crackuro!

Instructions

Kakuro is like a crossword: there are hints for rows and for columns. The difference is that these hints are numbers, not words, and they show what the sum of the numbers in the row to the right or the column below should be. Fill in the squares with single-digit numbers according to the hints, but you can't repeat digits in the same row or column: you can't have two nine's in the same row, for instance.

Adjust your puzzle's size and difficulty using the menu. If you want, you can play the game in a different base than decimal.
